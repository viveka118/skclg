📚 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

📅 Admission kab start hote hain?
Admission har saal April se start hote hain. Online aur offline dono mode available hain.
⏰ School timing kya hai?
School timing 8:00 AM se 2:00 PM tak hai (Monday to Saturday).
🎓 Admission ke liye kya documents chahiye?
Photo, Aadhar card, Transfer Certificate (agar class 6+), aur previous mark sheet.
💼 Uniform compulsory hai kya?
Haan, uniform sabhi students ke liye compulsory hai Monday to Saturday.
💰 Fees kis mode se pay kar sakte hain?
Aap Cash, UPI, Google Pay ya Online Bank Transfer se fees pay kar sakte hain.
🔁 Password bhool gaya ho to kya karein?
Login page par "Forgot Password" option par click karein. Aapka password email ke madhyam se prapt hoga.
📁 Study Material kaise milega?
Dashboard ke "Download Section" me aapko sabhi PDF files aur assignments milenge.
📊 Result kab aur kaise declare hota hai?
Result exam ke 10-15 din baad portal par update kiya jata hai. Aap dashboard se download kar sakte hain.
📱 Mobile ya laptop par portal kaise use karein?
Bas browser open karke student portal ka link kholen. Page mobile responsive hai.
