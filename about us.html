About Us

SK Inter College is committed to providing a supportive learning environment where students grow academically, socially, and morally. Our school believes in discipline, innovation, and holistic development.

We provide modern facilities like smart classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, sports grounds, a huge library, and cultural activity centers. Our goal is to shape responsible citizens with strong values.
